Our Services

Our ServiceS

At Safari Car Services, we take pride in offering a wide range of professional car wash and detailing packages tailored to meet your needs. Whether you're looking for a quick clean or a full luxury treatment, our team is here to ensure your car looks and feels brand new.

Engine Wash

Our Engine Wash service ensures your vehicle’s engine bay is free from dirt, oil, and grime buildup. We use safe, non corrosive cleaning agents and a gentle pressure wash to clean your engine without damaging sensitive components. A clean engine not only looks great but also improves performance and makes it easier to spot leaks or maintenance issues.

Body Wash

Our Body Wash service provides a thorough exterior cleaning using high quality shampoos and soft microfiber mitts to remove dirt, dust, and road grime. We pay special attention to detail, ensuring a spotless finish without scratching your paintwork. This service leaves your car looking fresh, clean, and well-maintained

Polish

Our Car Polish service restores your vehicle’s shine by removing minor scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation. Using premium polishing compounds and equipment, we bring out the natural gloss of your car’s paint, giving it a smooth, mirror-like finish. This treatment not only enhances the appearance but also prepares the surface for wax or sealant protection.

Vacuum

Our Interior Vacuum service ensures a clean and fresh cabin by thoroughly removing dust, dirt, crumbs, and debris from all interior surfaces. We vacuum seats, carpets, floor mats, and hard to reach areas to restore cleanliness and comfort to your vehicle’s interior.

Auto Wash

Our Auto Wash service provides a complete exterior clean for your vehicle using high quality, eco friendly products. We ensure your car is gently but effectively cleaned, removing dirt, grime, and road residue while protecting the paintwork and leaving a spotless shine.

Motorbikes

Our Motorbike Wash service is designed specifically for two wheelers. We use gentle, high-pressure cleaning and safe products to remove dirt, grease, and road grime from every part of your motorbike including wheels, chain guard, and undercarriage leaving it clean, shiny, and well-maintained.
